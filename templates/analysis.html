    Algorithm Info

    This dashboard uses a Q-learning agent to trade BTC automatically. The agent observes market prices and indicators, chooses actions (Buy, Sell, Hold), and learns by maximizing cumulative rewards.

    How it works:

    • Agent uses price & EMA indicators to determine market state.
    • It takes actions and receives rewards based on profit/loss.
    • Over time, it learns the optimal strategy to maximize PnL.
    • Backtesting allows you to see historical performance of the agent.

    Use the Controls panel to update data, train the agent, and backtest.
